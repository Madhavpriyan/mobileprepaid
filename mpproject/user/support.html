Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recharge my mobile phone?
To recharge your mobile phone, login to your account, select "New Recharge", enter your phone number, select your operator, choose a plan, and proceed to payment. Your recharge will be processed immediately upon successful payment.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets. You can view all available payment options during checkout.
How long does it take to process refunds?
Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days, depending on your payment method and bank processing times.
Can I schedule a recharge for later?
Yes, you can schedule recharges for future dates. Go to "Schedule Recharge", enter your details, select the date and time, and confirm. We'll automatically process the recharge at your specified time.
How do I check my transaction history?
You can view your complete transaction history by going to "Wallet" and then selecting the "Transaction History" tab. This shows all your recharges, wallet additions, and refunds.
